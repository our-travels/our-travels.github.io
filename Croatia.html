Plitvice Lakes

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Plitvice Lakes National Park is one of the oldest and largest national Parks in Croatia. Here you can enjoy the sights of 16 chain terraced lakes all joined and interconnected by waterfalls. This is a must visit for all trips to Croatia.
