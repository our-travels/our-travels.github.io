Tanegashima Beach

Where the Portugese army once arrived on the beaches of Tanegashima with weapons, these stunning beaches are now known for their surfing capabilities. Here you can watch the world go by while surrounded by complete and utter bliss and tranquility.

Tanegashima Space Center

Here if you're lucky you can watch a space rocket lift off, if not, you can simply explore and learn more about the wonders of Japanese space. It's Japan's largest space center and is now ran by Japanese JAXA.

Trees of Ureshino

In Ureshino you can enjoy numerous hikes, including the famous trail from South Korea that attracts hundreds of tourists every year. If you prefer to take a relaxing trip, you can visit the numerous onsens available.
